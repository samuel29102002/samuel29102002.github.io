02 · How I build

Reproducible or it didn't happen.

Three rules I keep coming back to.

01

Boots on a laptop

No paid infra, no proprietary feed, no GPU. If a peer can't reproduce a result on a clean shell, it's a story, not a project.

02

Benchmarks before charts

Ops/sec, ms/inference, % accuracy. Numbers go in the README before any pretty visualisation. Charts decorate; benchmarks decide.

03

German precision, indie tempo

Type-checked public APIs, files under 500 lines, tests where it matters — but shipped on the timeline of someone with a piano practice in the evening.
