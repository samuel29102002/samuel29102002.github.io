About

Quiet ambition,
loud spreadsheets.

I grew up in southern Germany and never really left — Stuttgart and Tübingen are the two pins on the map I keep coming back to. The other pins are added one trip at a time.

My week is split between corporate governance dashboards (compliance, FTE/VZK gap analytics, automation in Google Apps Script) and after-hours work that looks suspiciously like a quant research desk: order books, derivatives pricing, sensor-data ML, energy forecasting.

02 · How I think

Three rules
I keep close.

If a project violates all three at once, I scrap it.

Evidence over opinion

Causal inference framing — even outside academia. "What would have happened otherwise?" is a better default than "what looks correlated?".

Small enough to finish

A repo that boots in a minute and a spec that fits in a paragraph beats a roadmap with eighteen quarters and zero releases.

Quiet by default

German engineering posture: ship the thing, write the README, move on. Marketing is a side effect of having actually finished it.
