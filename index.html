Quantitative data science.
Tübingen.

Master's at Eberhard Karls Universität Tübingen. B.Sc. from DHBW Stuttgart with TRUMPF SE.

I study quantitative data science at the University of Tübingen. Before that, a dual B.Sc. at DHBW Stuttgart with TRUMPF SE, including six months at TRUMPF Inc. in Farmington, Connecticut.

Most of my work sits at the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and finance. Currently a Werkstudent at the Schwarz Group alongside the Master's.

Recent projects: a Rust matching engine with a DeepLOB classifier, a Bundesbank-curve-based pricing pipeline for a German Express certificate, and IMU sensor ML on embedded hardware.
