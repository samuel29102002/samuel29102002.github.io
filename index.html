01 · About

Engineering for the things
that don't tolerate noise.

Compliance dashboards by day. Order books, derivatives and sensors by night. Same posture: precision, reproducibility, evidence.

I'm Samuel — born in Germany on the day quants discovered the volatility smile was not a smile at all. I work at the intersection of data engineering, econometrics and quantitative finance, with a soft spot for things that compute fast and break slowly.

By day I build governance and FTE-gap analytics for the Schwarz Group (Lidl · Kaufland · PreZero) — Google Apps Script, Python, openpyxl, and a Bloomberg-grade obsession with the last decimal.

By night I write Rust matching engines, price German Express certificates against Bundesbank Svensson curves, and run causal inference under the Rubin potential-outcomes framework. Then I cook on a budget.
